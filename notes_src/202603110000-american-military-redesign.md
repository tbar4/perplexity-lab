The problem in American military is a lack of imagination. We require sweeping redesign of the American military. We are currently built around a small number of large, expensive, complex, intricate, heavily manned and hard replace platforms to a military built around large numbers of smaller, lower cost, expendable, and highly autonomous machines. Simply it should be a military to find less by the strength of quantities of its platforms, and by the efficacy, speed, flexibility, adaptability, and overall dynamism of its kill chain. This will require heavy investments and big data in AI and partnering with Silicon Valley instead of defense contractors that have financial incentive to keep selling large expensive and complex platforms.
