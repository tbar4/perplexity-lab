Space systems are crucial for maintaining spacepower. They are responsible for nuclear arsenals being effective, missile defense, and gathering information. Space systems allow for extensive monitoring of Earth with relatively few resources. We define space systems as assets that either exist in suborbital or outer space or ground control systems, including launch facilities, for these assets. Space asset organizations are organizations that build, operate, maintain, or own space systems.
