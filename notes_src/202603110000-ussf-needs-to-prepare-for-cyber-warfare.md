USSF CSO General B. Chance Saltzman is warning that the USSF needs to prepare for cyber conflicts in space. He goes as far to mention that Space and Cyber are inextricably linked and that a failure or breach in their cyber systems compromises all of the USSF. If data integrity cannot be assured between the satellite and ground station, then they're absolutely useless. Cyber attacks present a cheap and easy way to degrade American Military force.
