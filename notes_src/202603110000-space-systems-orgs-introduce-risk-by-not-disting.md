Organizations that create space systems generally don't distinguish between internal IT teams and Operational IT. This means that IT professionals that may have been trained to help troubleshoot network problems on windows are not seen as any different than someone who is trained to understand the vulnerabilities of a space system written in a custom linux distro\[\^1\].
