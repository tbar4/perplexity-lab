Stuxnet is considered to be the world's first cyber weapon. It was a computer virus (allegedly) created by joint US-Israeli forces in which the virus silently occupied PLC's in an Iranian Nuclear facility that spun centrifuges of nuclear refinement material out of control and causing them to blow up.
