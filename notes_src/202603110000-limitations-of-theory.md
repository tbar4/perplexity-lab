It is impossible to replicate every singe variable in the real world, so theory is limited by observation of the known knowns and because of this, theory has to be simplified and abstracted to an extent. Theory does rely on empirical data, but it also can call upon other theory in order to complete its logic. Theories are bound to be wrong, but it is the responsibility of theorists to make them as little wrong as possible.
