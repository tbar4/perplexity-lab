Many commercial and military systems rely on space systems to conduct their most fundamental operations. Agribusiness relies on weather and climate satellites to plan and adapt to changing conditions in the short and long term. The military has been making use of intelligence satellites for as nearly long as we have been launching to space. Transportation like, cars, buses, planes, and trains all rely on information provided from GPS which then gets fed into other systems that help manage safety and traffic. Many systems also rely on space systems for accurate time and synchronization like network data transmission and the financial sector.
